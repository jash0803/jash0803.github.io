Construction Tech

NirmaanX

Funding: Received Rs. 1.5 lakh in funding from SSIP (Student Startup and Innovation Policy), Gujarat.

Making Buildings Structurally Honest. NirmaanX is a professional structural inspection platform built for construction teams.

It helps teams start and finish site visits, capture every issue with photos, deliver polished PDF reports via email, and get AI-powered analysis insights across the full inspection workflow.

Chrome Extension

Social Vault

Social Vault is a browser extension that stores all your social media links in one place, making them easy to access and paste while filling out forms.

  • Social Links Manager: Add, view, and manage your social media links easily.
  • Personal Details Section: Store personal information like name, phone number, email, address, and pincode.
  • Profile Sharing: Generate a dynamic profile card to share your information.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Track total clicks and time saved using your profile.
